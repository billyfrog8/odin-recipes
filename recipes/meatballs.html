Meatball Recipe

Description

These delicous meatballs are the perfect dinner to prepare for your family, or to have lunch for the week The meatballs take approximately one hour to cook. Pair the meatballs with a creamy pasta sauce and boxed or home-made spaghetti or noodles for an exquisite dining experience.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground beef(leaner for firmer meatballs)
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 4 Oz butter
  • 3 tsp italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. mix the ground beef with butter and salf in a large bowl
  2. move the beef to a large saucepan on high heat
  3. cook for 5-7 minutes, or until desired texture. stir occasionally
  4. top with italian seasoning and enjoy
