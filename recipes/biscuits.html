Biscuit Recipe

Description

These delicous bisuits will melt in your mouth and absolutely delight your taste buds

Ingredients

  • 1 lball-purpose flower
  • 4 Oz butter with salt
  • 3 tbsp baking soda

Instructions

  1. add ingredients to a large bowl, knead thouroughly
  2. preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  3. line a baking sheet with parchment paper, cook bisuits until golden brown
