Pizza Recipe

Description

This pizza recipe is the perfect mix of New York crust and pixie dust

Ingredients

  • Flower
  • salt
  • active yeast

Instructions

  1. Let yeast rise in lukewarm water
  2. Mix flower and salt with yeast
  3. Form a ball with the dough
  4. Top with cheese and sauce then cook in oven at 425 degrees for 8 minutes
